A short Biography of Pablo Picasso

  1. Pablo Picasso was born on October 25,1881 in a city of Malaga town, spain
  2. His real name was Pablo Diego jose Fransisco de Paula
  3. He received his name when he was baptized
  4. His long name came from a list of saint and relatives
  5. His first word was "lapiz" the Spanish word meaning pencil
  6. His father was an artist, Art professor an museum curator
  7. Pablo completed his first painting when he was nine years old, the title of the painting was Le picodor . the image of a man riding on a horse of a bullfight
  8. He studied at the royal Academy of San Fernando
  9. Picasso first job was with an art dealer name pere menach
  10. Picasso was the co-founder in the movement know as cubism
  11. Picasso had four children with three different women
  12. He was married twice
  13. Picasso passed away in April 8,1973 in mougins, France.
